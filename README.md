# 🏥 Clinic Management System  
Dr. J. Hudyono Clinic – Bintaro Permai, Jakarta Selatan

---

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on developing a clinic management system to improve patient registration, appointment scheduling, and medical record management.

Previously, clinic operations were handled manually, resulting in long queues, unstructured patient data, and difficulties in tracking medical records and appointments.

The system is designed to streamline clinic operations, improve service efficiency, and ensure better data organization.

---

## 🎯 Key Features
- Patient Registration Management  
- Appointment Scheduling  
- Medical Record Management  
- Doctor & Staff Management  
- Queue Management
